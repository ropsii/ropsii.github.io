My design process

I’ve always tailored my process to the structure of the organization. There are specific adjustments to how any team ships software, but I’ve been following this very same method since forever. How you get to this loop, with what tools, is less relevant.

  1. Design with available information
  2. Ship and observe
  3. Iterate and repeat

During my tenure at Semaphore CI, I developed a simple illustration system to make the flow less intimidating. This was especially helpful during onboarding, to breathe life into dry, technical explanations.

A great share of my efforts is designing at the structural level, consolidating how different systems interact with each other, and how that understanding translates to value for the users and the company.
