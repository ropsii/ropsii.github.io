My design process

This is roughly a method I’ve been using throughout my career, regardless of the size of the team I was part of.

  1. Design with the information you got
  2. Ship and observe
  3. Listen to users, listen to colleagues, look at numbers
  4. Use a healthy ratio of intuition and numbers
  5. Unless you’re Amazon, then always go with numbers
  6. Understand a broader business context
  7. Everybody in the company shapes the UX; Customer support knows product flaws better than the product team, use that knowledge
  8. Iterate with new findings
  9. Repeat indefinitely
  10. Leave when you’re sick of it
