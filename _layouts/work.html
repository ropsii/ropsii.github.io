Most recent work

From 2016-21, I was a principal designer at Semaphore CI, a tool that helps developers test and deploy their code. I was in command of product design and strategy.

In a super-contributor role, I was responsible for a wide array of duties. From figuring out what to build next to designing and shipping features.

Semaphore is a small, 25-person team. I was balancing between product strategy, user feedback synthesis, UI design, and production-ready code.

I developed a simple illustration system to make the flow less intimidating. This was especially helpful during onboarding, to breathe life into dry, technical explanations.

A great portion of my efforts is designing at the structural level, consolidating how different systems interact with each other, and how that understanding translates to value for the users and the company.
